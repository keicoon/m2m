📖 How to Play

Minesweeper is a classic logic puzzle game. Your goal is to uncover all the safe squares without detonating any hidden mines.

  • Left-Click: Reveal a square. If it's a mine, you lose!
  • Right-Click (or Flag Mode): Place a flag 🚩 on squares you suspect are mines.
  • Numbers: A number shows how many mines are hidden in the adjacent 8 squares. Use logic to figure out the safe spots!

⚔️ Multiplayer (P2P) Mode

Challenge a friend in real-time! In P2P mode, you both receive the exact same board. The first player to clear all safe squares wins. If you hit a mine, your opponent instantly wins!

* No server is used. You connect directly using a generated Connection ID.
